A Private Social Club in West Midtown Atlanta.

The Dark Room is a place for hustlers, thinkers, creatives, and doers.

Getting inside isn’t easy. You have to know someone with a key, and there are only a few keys around.

Once inside, you’ll find a large lounge with all the amenities you need for entertaining clients, coworkers, or friends; and two smaller rooms for meetings, conversation, or a friendly game of cards. There are also multiple TVs with all the streaming packages, so watching the game or binging that show is never a problem.

Whether it’s for solace from the day’s chaos or hosting a few friends for a whisky tasting, The Dark Room always delivers.
